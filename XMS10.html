XMS10

Threat Level: None

Looks:

XMS10 imitates a human, taking the form of a white male that is 14 years of age.


Behavior:

XMS10 acts similarly to other humans, blending in easily. It acts positive, smiling for most of its conversations with other humans. It rarely expresses feelings of anger.

If upset:

If upset at a human, XMS10 says one of two responses.

1. "Let's just not." - Response kindly telling the collocutor to stop whatever it is they're doing. 2. "Can we not?" - A more irritated response asking the collocutor to stop.

However, if extremely agitated, it lightly slaps the person it is annoyed at. If the person is wearing shoelaces, XMS10 will untie them.
